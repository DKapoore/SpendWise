Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find the answer to your question in our FAQ section.

Is SpendWise really free to use?
Yes, SpendWise is completely free with no hidden costs or premium tiers. We believe financial management tools should be accessible to everyone.
How do I export my expense data?
You can export your expense data as a PDF report by clicking the "Export PDF" button in the application. The report will include all your entries and calculations.
Is my financial data stored on your servers?
No, all your data remains in your browser. We don't store any of your financial information on our servers, which helps protect your privacy.
