Getting Started

How do I start using SpendWise? â–¼

Using SpendWise is simple! Just visit our homepage and you can start tracking expenses immediately. No registration or download is required. Simply enter your total budget amount, then click the "+" button to add expenses as you spend.

Do I need to create an account? â–¼

No, SpendWise works without requiring an account. All your data is stored locally in your browser. This means you can start using the application immediately without any sign-up process.

Is there a mobile app? â–¼

SpendWise is a web application that works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access it through your mobile browser. While there isn't a dedicated mobile app in app stores, the web app is optimized for mobile use and can be added to your home screen for quick access.

Features

How do I export my data as PDF? â–¼

To export your expense data as a PDF, simply click the "Export PDF" button in the application. This will generate a comprehensive report including all your expenses, descriptions, and the calculated remaining balance. The PDF will be downloaded directly to your device.

Can I edit or delete expenses? â–¼

Yes, you can delete any expense by clicking the "-" button next to it. To edit an expense, you can directly modify the description or amount in the input fields. The remaining balance will update automatically as you make changes.

What happens if I exceed my budget? â–¼

If your expenses exceed your budget, SpendWise will display the amount you've overspent in red. This visual indicator helps you quickly identify when you've gone over budget so you can adjust your spending accordingly.

Troubleshooting

I lost my data. How can I recover it? â–¼

Since SpendWise stores data locally in your browser, clearing your browser data or using private browsing can result in data loss. We recommend regularly exporting your data using the PDF export feature to maintain backups. Unfortunately, we cannot recover lost data as we don't store it on our servers.

The application isn't working correctly. What should I do? â–¼

If you're experiencing issues with SpendWise, try these steps:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Clear your browser cache and try again
  3. Try using a different browser
  4. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
If problems persist, please contact our support team with details about the issue.

Can I use SpendWise on multiple devices? â–¼

Yes, you can use SpendWise on multiple devices. However, since data is stored locally on each device, your budget and expenses won't sync automatically between devices. You would need to manually enter the same information on each device or use the PDF export/import feature to transfer data.

Privacy & Security

Is my financial data secure? â–¼

Yes, your financial data is secure because we don't store it on our servers. All your budget information and expense data remains in your browser. This means we never have access to your financial information, providing an additional layer of privacy and security.

Do you sell my data to third parties? â–¼

No, we do not sell your data to third parties. In fact, we don't collect your financial data at all since it remains in your browser. We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the application, but this doesn't include any personal or financial information.

How is SpendWise free? â–¼

SpendWise is completely free to use. We believe that basic financial management tools should be accessible to everyone. The application is supported through non-intrusive advertisements and the dedication of our development team. We are committed to keeping the core functionality free forever.
